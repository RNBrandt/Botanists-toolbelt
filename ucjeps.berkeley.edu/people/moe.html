 Richard L. Moe

 Dr. Richard L. Moe, now retired, served as Manager of Collections Data from 2000 to 2013, responsible for
  • the specimen data base (SMASCH): for coordinating data entry, for database maintenance, and for arranging data retrieval
  • writing and maintaining the Herbaria web pages.
  • the Jepson Fieldbooks Digitization Project.
  • computer aspects of the Jepson Interchange
  • construction and maintenance of the Consortium of California Herbaria
  • providing hardware and software service/assistance to the staff of the Herbaria
  • editing Constancea, the online journal of the Herbaria.
