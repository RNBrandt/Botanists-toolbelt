The Baldwin Lab

  • ABOUT THE BALDWIN LAB
    Dr. Baldwin's research group resides within the Jepson Herbarium (where he is the Curator) and the Department of Integrative Biology (where he is a Professor). Students in Dr. Baldwin's lab receive their degrees through the Department of Integrative Biology.

    The Baldwin lab is involved in research on systematics and floristics of vascular plants, with an emphasis on the California flora. Students and postdoctoral researchers in the lab have undertaken studies on a wide diversity of evolutionary and biogeographic questions in various plant groups, with most studies to date involving a phylogenetic component.
